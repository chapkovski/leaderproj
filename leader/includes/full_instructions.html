You are part of a team that has been tasked with producing 6 forecasts for ACME Inc’s monthly sales.

 

Team configuration

•       Each team will have 3 members: 1 team leader assigned randomly and 2 team participants. These roles will remain constant until the end of the task.

•       All the team members must discuss their opinions about the forecast in the team chat.

•       Once the discussion—or the time limit—is over, all team members will submit an individual forecast.

•       Importantly, only the leader’s final forecast will count for the group scoring. The leader will have the opportunity to make a final decision in a separate screen that will show all individual forecasts.

 

The task (estimated duration: 45mins)

•       Before starting the forecasting task, you must watch a short video(s) and answer some simple comprehension questions.

•       Your team needs to provide 6 forecasts for monthly sales in 6 rounds.

•       The process is as follows:

•       Round 1: You get 48 months of actuals as input and must forecast the 49th month.

•       Round 2: You get the 49 months of actuals (including the comparison between your round 1 forecast and the actuals) and must forecast the 50th month.

•       Round 3-6: Same logic as above, you get one more month of actuals and need to forecast the next month.

•       The first round will last {{session.config.round_one_minutes}} mins to allow for some team introductions, whereas the other rounds will last {{session.config.other_round_minutes}} mins maximum. The next round starts only when all 3 members have submitted, and the leader has made the final decision.

•       After all forecasts are done, you will have to complete 2 short surveys.

Measuring performance

•       After each round you will see the group forecast (leader’s decision) compared to the actuals so you can discuss potential adjustments in the next round.

•       Once the 6 rounds are completed, your team will get an accuracy score using MAPE (mean absolute percentage error):

•       less than 20% MAPE value means a good or highly accurate forecast,

•       between 20-50% reasonable,

•       and more than 50% is inaccurate.

•       Importantly, the person assigned with the role of leader is the solely responsible for deciding the final forecast and submitting it on behalf of the team.

•       Your team needs to provide 6 forecasts for monthly sales, one month each time during 6 rounds.

•       Once the 6 rounds are completed, your team will get an accuracy score using MAPE (mean absolute percentage error):

•       less than 20% MAPE value means a good or highly accurate forecast,

•       between 20-50% reasonable,

•       and more than 50% is inaccurate.

•       You will have 3mins to submit each forecast. If you don’t submit before 3mins you will get a 0 value as forecast.

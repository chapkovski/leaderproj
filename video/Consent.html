CONSENT FORM

In this study, we are interested in how people make group decisions. Please make sure to read all instructions carefully before selecting and submitting a response. There are also comprehension check questions before entering the actual survey.

By agreeing to participate in this study, you confirm that you are 18 or older. Your decision to participate in this study is voluntary. Please note that you are free to withdraw from the study at any time. Refusal to take part in this study, or withdrawing from it, will involve no penalty or loss of benefits that you would otherwise receive. There are no risks involved in this study that you would not encounter in daily life.

Please note that our studies are purely for academic purposes. The results are available to the public in research journals, books and other academic publications. Your data will remain confidential and will be treated anonymously. In fact, we are only interested in aggregated responses and not individual responses. The storage of any non-anonymized data follows the procedures and policies indicated by IE Data Privacy Offices (DataPrivacyOffice@ie.edu) for the access and storage of such data for compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and/or any other applicable Spanish or European Regulation.

If you have any questions pertaining to this research or your rights as a participant, you have the right to contact the researcher:

  • Dr. Matthias Seifert
  • IE Business School
  • Email: matthias.seifert@ie.edu

You will have an opportunity to receive a complete explanation of the research and its purposes following completion of the study.

If you are satisfied with the information outlined above and agree to participate in this research study, please click on the "I agree to participate" button below to continue. Please print this page if you would like to keep a record of this consent form.
